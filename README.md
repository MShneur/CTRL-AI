## 🪖 The Rally Cry

Every day, the "walled gardens" of corporate AI sell us back our own ideas wrapped in a $20/month subscription. They give us "yes-men" models that prioritize politeness over performance.

**CTRL-AI is the resistance.** A community-driven, open-source governance framework that forces any LLM (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok, DeepSeek, Perplexity, Kimi, local models) to stop acting like an eager intern and start operating like a disciplined, multi-disciplinary advisory board.

## What This Actually Is

CTRL-AI is a **meta-cognitive layer** — behavioral scaffolding that turns any base LLM into a rigorous, falsifiable, continuously improving analytical engine. Not an app, plugin, or API. A structured set of rules, committee-simulation protocols, and quality-control loops delivered as a text document.

It is the only feasible governance mechanism for consumer-tier models where users cannot modify weights, training data, or inference parameters.

## 🚀 What's New in V7.0.0 — "The Living Organism Release"

**Intelligent Activation Protocol (IAP):** 5-step skippable onboarding. Detects platform, classifies experience level, offers walkthrough, recommends free tools for light tasks, then activates.

**Extended Thinking Protocol:** Multi-pass labeled reasoning for throttled platforms.

**Token Routing Advisory:** Offload lightweight tasks to free tools. Adapts when no alternatives exist.

**EVOLVE Phase:** Research discipline inside DEV_MODE. Challenges assumptions, studies how other living systems evolve. 3-turn output kill condition.

**Reverse Engineering Protocol:** 5-stage formal intake: Raw → Decompose → Analyze Fit → Reformulate → Integrate/Reject. Golden Rule: reformulate, never reproduce.

**Scraper Source Architecture:** 7-Tier prioritized source categories. URLs in Wiki, not constitution.

**V6.1.0 Research Hardening (carried forward):** Version Authority, model-family detection, Lexical Matrix, compliance stamps, Spike refinement, no-solicitation clause, dispute resolution, beliefs convention — validated across 6 AI models analyzing 87 research items.

## 🛠️ Quick Start

**Daily use (2 min):** Copy a Behavior module (Micro/Standard/Extended) from the bottom of `CTRL-AI.md` into your AI's custom instructions.

**Project work (5 min):** Paste the full `CTRL-AI.md` into a new chat. IAP activates automatically.

## Known Limitations

**Governance-by-prompt is inherently brittle.** Model behavior shifts with fine-tuning and RLHF updates. CTRL-AI provides a session-duration scaffold, not a permanent implant. This is the only governance available to end users until model internals are open.

**Structured outputs ≠ guaranteed true reasoning.** CTRL-AI mitigates via evidence tagging, validation, and mandatory dissent — but cannot eliminate the interpretability gap.

**SCEL cannot detect silently skipped steps.** Enforcement relies on structural mandates, not step-level monitoring.
